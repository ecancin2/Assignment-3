The Breed Scanner

An App to Scan your Pet's Breed!

Welcome to the breed scanner website! Here, we will show you what our wonderful app can do! Ever wondered what breed your pet or a stray cat or dog was out of sheer curiosity? Using our trusty pet scanner, just scan your pet's face, and bingo, that's all the steps you need! In addition, you get facts and ways to improve your pet's lifestyle! Be in awe at the extreme convenience this app can hold!



Current Pet Owners

If you currently own a pet, you may want to be informed on the type of breed your pet is. By scanning your pet and getting information on your breed, you can find out the right type of dog food for them that are recommended by veternarians for puppies/kittens or adults. In addition, you can find how much they should eat depending on their age, how much should they be walked if they're dog, how long they should be indoor or outdoor if they're a cat, and what type of vaccinations each breed should get.

New Pet Owners

If you are interseted in adopting a new pet, whether a cat or a dog, you may want to know the details of the breed of each pet that you are highly considering in getting. Of course, an alternative method can be to use Google instead, and write down the info there is. However, with the app, you don't need to remember what you googled. You can simply go to the pet store, scan any dog or cat you identify as the ones you are interested in, and get all the info you need about them when making an informed decision. You can know about their personalities, whether they're indoor or outdoor, etc.

Curious People

If you are very curious about stray dogs or cats that are around your neighborhood, or curious when visiting friends or family who happen to have pets with them, you may want to satiate that curiosity. Instead of trying to Google the breed that you think is correct from a simple observation, with our app, you can get the result you desire at the press of a button! Just press to scan the pet's face, and now you have solved the mystery of the pet's breed!



Solution

The app would be a scanning device where you scan the face of your pet or someone else's pet, as well as a stray, and would show you the correct breed based on their facial features. There would be a database storing info for every cat and dog breed possible, guaranteeing the right breed for your pet.

Pricing

This app would be a one-time purchase on the app store, and the price would be around $1.99, or $2.99 at the maximum. It would be reasonably cheap to get, an would grant you unlimited scanning power!
